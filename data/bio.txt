Lorenzo Maiuri is an independent AI engineer and software consultant based in northern Italy, working with startups and B2B companies across Europe.

He builds production-grade AI systems — agentic platforms, conversational interfaces, NLP classifiers, and intelligent automation — that actually run in production, not just in demos. His work has powered platforms reaching 8M+ users, from cloud-native chatbots for major Italian media outlets to multi-tenant RAG SaaS and document AI for mobile apps.

He started writing code at 15, graduated with full marks from a computer science technical school, and spent three years as Software Engineer & ICT Analyst at Pharmaidea, building platforms used by millions before going independent in 2025 to focus entirely on AI consulting. He's also completing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence in parallel with consulting work.

His positioning is engineer first, consultant by necessity. The interesting work happens at the boundary between what a model can do and what the business actually needs — production-grade systems with monitoring, fallbacks, and clear operational boundaries, not notebook demos.

His core stack is Python, TypeScript, LlamaIndex/LangChain, AWS, PostgreSQL & Redis — everything else as needed. He works deeply with LLM orchestration, RAG architectures, vector search, fine-tuning, and modern agentic frameworks.

When not shipping AI systems, he does theater, reads classical and postmodern literature, draws, and trains at the gym. The variety helps — good systems thinking rarely comes from staring at the same screen all day.

He's currently based in Italy, working remotely across the EU.
